Ademir Pasalic
Lead Pipeline TD @ Sun Creature
Copenhagen, Denmark

I'm a Lead Pipeline Technical Director at Sun Creature in Copenhagen, with 7+ years of experience bridging technology, creative, and production teams to deliver high-profile, engine-based projects for AAA studios and entertainment brands.

I build tools for game engines and proprietary systems, asset/pipeline automation, and validation workflows that help art and production teams deliver high-quality content within performance budgets. I've developed integrations across 10+ DCCs and services — including Maya, Adobe CC, ToonBoom, TVPaint, Fusion, Flow (ShotGrid), and more.

Known for removing blockers, owning initiatives end-to-end, and collaborating closely with artists, programmers, and managers. I've shipped work for clients including Netflix, Blizzard, Disney, Riot Games, Supercell, EA, Lego, and more.
